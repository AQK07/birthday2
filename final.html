I have stood beside her through many eras of our kingdom. She is not just our leader — she is the face of kindness, hope and love. In many moments she has been the guiding light not only for me, but for all our citizens. She is truly a being of pure light.

I am but a simple man, not what you would call gifted. Yet she saw something special within me and took me in as her advisor. She has since then continued to show me how amazing she is every single day. I am so thankful to be her advisor.
